RAPHA FAITH FOUNDATION is a CAC Registered NON GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATION whose aim is to promote welfare activities in breaking the circle of poverty and social isolation among the poor, helpless and the Venerable, to create a network for social development and integration of underprivileged individuals, groups and communities, to organize medical outreaches, seminars, workshops, crusades, counselling to the general population in urban and rural settings and also to collaborate with other NGO'S working towards similar goals
Our mission is to enhance the well-being of the underprivileged and vulnerable populations by breaking the cycle of poverty and social isolation through comprehensive medical care and welfare activities. We aim to build a robust network dedicated to social development, ensuring that individuals, groups, and communities who are marginalized and in need receive the support, resources, and integration necessary to thrive.
To create a world where every individual, regardless of their socioeconomic status, has equitable access to essential medical care and opportunities for social integration. We envision a society where the cycle of poverty and social isolation is broken, and all individuals are empowered to reach their full potential in a supportive and inclusive community.
